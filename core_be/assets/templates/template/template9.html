Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about orders, shipping and returns. Still stuck? Our Bristol-based team replies to every message within a day.

Message our team
How long will my order take to arrive?

Standard UK orders arrive within two to three working days, and orders over £75 ship free. Express next-day delivery is available at checkout before 2pm. You will get a tracking link by email the moment your parcel leaves our warehouse.

What is your return and exchange policy?

Changed your mind? Send anything back within 60 days for a full refund or a free size exchange. Items should be unworn with tags attached. Request a prepaid label from your account and drop the parcel at any post office — refunds land within five working days of it reaching us.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes — we ship to more than 40 countries across Europe, North America and Asia-Pacific. Duties and taxes are calculated at checkout so there are no surprise fees on delivery. International orders typically arrive within five to eight working days.

How do I track my order?

Every order comes with a tracking link sent to your email as soon as it ships. You can also see live status under Orders in your account. If tracking has not updated after 48 hours, reach out and we will chase the courier for you.

Which payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards, plus Apple Pay, Google Pay, PayPal and Klarna for paying in three interest-free instalments. Checkout is protected with bank-grade encryption and we never store your card details.

How should I care for my pieces?

Each product page lists full care instructions, and a printed card ships in the box. As a rule, wash cottons cool and line dry to keep their shape, and treat leather goods with a natural conditioner twice a year. Look after them well and they are built to last a decade.
